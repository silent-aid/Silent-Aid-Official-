📌 About Us

Silent Aid is a youth-led movement delivering emergency aid and raising awareness through symbolic, powerful content. We believe that silence does not mean inaction — and our silence echoes louder through impact.

🎯 Current Campaign: Echoes of the Unseen

This campaign uses creative storytelling — reels, carousels, and symbolic art — to raise awareness for Palestine in ways that bypass suppression and ignite global empathy.
